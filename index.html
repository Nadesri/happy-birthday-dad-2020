You are smart, you are kind, you are giving, you are loving. When my coworkers and friends ask me who my hero is, it's no question that you (and dad) are. I look back to when I was a little crybaby brat and I don't know what you had in you to still practice so much love and patience with me. Thank you, mama.

You've taught me so much in all the stages of my life, and while there were awkward stages or moments of ambiguity with where I was going with my life, you were always there cheering me on: from teaching me not to throw toys at adults and making me sit in the corner for time-out, spending time with me after school to help me with my chemistry homework, to helping me with my college applications, guiding how I handle difficult situations and relationships, walking me through my confusing taxes, to supporting my big decision to move to Switzerland.
